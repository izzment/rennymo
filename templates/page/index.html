Renny Mo

The Excellent Compère

Morenikeji, also known as RennyMo, is a multipotentialite and social entrepreneur, an award-winning broadcast journalist, and an expert in public speaking and development communications strategy. She serves as a corporate event compère and is a passionate advocate for Sustainable Development Goals 5, 3, and 13.

RennyMo has worked with local and international organizations across West Africa, moderating panels, hosting events as a Master of Ceremony, and developing and executing effective development communications strategies. Her contributions have earned her commendations from esteemed organizations, including the United Nations Nigeria, German Missions in Nigeria, YouthhubAfrica, ActionAid Nigeria, West Africa Civil Society Institute (WASSI), NGYouthSDGs, and JayFm Jos.

As a women and gender justice advocate, she is the Executive director at The GLW Community, alumna of the Basic Education Media Fellowship (BEMF) and The African MasterClass Series Cohort 4.
